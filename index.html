Experts in Fire Safety Compliance Solutions

At Independent Accredited Practitioner Fire Safety, we excel in providing professional, reliable and unbiased high calibre fire safety compliance consulting services to building owners, strata managers and fire services providers.

We are QUALIFIED accredited fire safety practitioners with extensive experience in the fire safety industry to assess all the 36 essential fire safety measures in accordance with their minimum standards of performance.

Led by our Qualified APFS Principal who has a Master of Fire Safety Engineering and Mechanical Engineering degrees, our independent team has a depth of experience in providing fire safety compliance and certification services to customers across all industries and all types of buildings — including office & commercial buildings, apartments, healthcare & nursing homes, warehouses & factories, education and other institutional facilities.

Trust our experienced professional team to deliver precise and effective fire safety compliance solutions for your premises.
